The following is a collection of analytical type data projects in which I experiment with different techniques, methods, and algorithms to reveal insights for why the given data is the way it is.

Winnipeg Cropland Dataset

Experimentation with sensor dataset taken from the UCI Machine Learning Repository. It was made from the output of two sensors, one optical via RapidEye satellite and one polarimetric via Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Syntheic Apeture Radar system, during two flyovers of fields of crops on dates that were 9 days apart. Each entry consists of the syncronized outputs of both sensors on both of the days over the same crop and the classification of the cropland is codified into classes 1-7.
